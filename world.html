What We Believe

The principles behind every collection

Made Slowly

We release four collections a year, not fifty-two. Every piece is produced in limited runs, made-to-order where possible, to reduce waste and overproduction.

Made Traceably

Every garment can be traced back to the atelier and material source that made it. We publish our supplier list and visit every workshop we work with.

Made to Last

We favor natural fibers — silk, linen, wool, cashmere — chosen for durability first. Our Care Guide and repair service help pieces outlive trends.
